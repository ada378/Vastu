Vastu Interior Design

Design your home according to Vastu Shastra principles for harmony, positivity, and prosperity

Kitchen

Heart of the home - Kitchen should be in Southeast direction

  • Facing East while cooking
  • Gas stove in Southeast
  • Wash basin in East/Northeast
  • Avoid kitchen in Northeast

Bedroom

Rest and rejuvenation - Best in Southwest direction

  • Head towards South/Southwest
  • Place bed in Southwest corner
  • Avoid mirrors facing bed
  • Use soothing colors

Main Door

Gateway to fortune - Best in North, East or Northeast

  • North - Growth & prosperity
  • East - Health & happiness
  • Northeast - Spiritual energy
  • Avoid South & West facing

Pooja Room

Sacred space - Northeast corner is most auspicious

  • Always in Northeast corner
  • Facing East or North
  • Keep clean and decorated
  • Use brass/copper utensils

Bathroom

Purification zone - Should be in Northwest or West

  • Avoid Northeast bathroom
  • Northwest is ideal location
  • Keep doors closed always
  • Proper ventilation must

Living Room

Family gathering - North or East facing is best

  • North - Career & business
  • East - Knowledge & health
  • Heavy furniture in South/West
  • Use bright, warm colors

Staircase

Energy flow - Best in South or West area

  • Avoid Northeast corner
  • Clockwise winding preferred
  • Number of steps - odd numbers
  • No stairs in center of house

Lighting

Positive energy - Proper light placement matters

  • Bright lights in Northeast
  • Warm lights in South/West
  • Avoid dark corners
  • Use natural light more

Vastu Quick Tips

Direction Matters: Northeast is the most sacred direction for spiritual energy. Always keep this area clean, well-lit, and clutter-free for maximum positive energy flow in your home.
